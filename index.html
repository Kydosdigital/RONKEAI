Simple. Compassionate. Effective.

RONKE uses the technology elderly patients already know: the telephone.

01

Regular Check-In Calls

After hospital discharge, RONKE calls patients regularly to check on their recovery. Simple, friendly questions about how they're feeling—no buttons to press, no apps to download.

02

AI-Powered Care Intelligence

Our proprietary conversation analysis identifies early warning signs that a patient may be deteriorating. Clinically validated questions detect symptoms before they become emergencies.

03

Clinical Team Alerts

Nurses and clinicians access a dashboard showing which patients need urgent follow-up. Early intervention prevents crisis and saves lives.
